How to Write the URL to Your NFC Tag

1
Download NFC Tools — free app by wakdev on iOS or Android.
2
Tap "Copy URL" above to copy your emergency link.
3
In NFC Tools, open Write → Add a record → URL/URI. Paste the link.
4
Tap Write and hold your phone to the tag. Anyone who taps it now sees your full emergency profile instantly.
⚠️ Keep your tag updated if medications or contacts change. Consider wearing it on a wristband or attaching it to your ID/wallet for fastest access by first responders.
